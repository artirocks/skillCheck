What is SkillCheckBlockchain Platform?

Decentralized Blockchain Application Platform and Services

SkillCheck Blockchain Platform is a private blockchain service built on top of Proof of Professional Stake consensus protocol (PoPS). PoPS is designed for enterprise grade blockchain applications. The SkillCheck Blockchain Platform is fully compatible with EVM based Solidity smart contracts.

The SkillCheck Blockchain Platform provides businesses and developers a fast and efficient way to develop and deploy blockchain applications. Taking the lessons learned from building the EchoLink service, we distill all the necessary components of making successful blockchain applications and offer these components as services.

With SkillCheck Blockchain Platform, Employers may take advantage of all the benefits brought by blockchain while not being entangled by the low level issues associated with blockchain technology.

What is SkillCheck Service? (Our Core)

SkillCheck is a blockchain based system that provides verified education, skill, and work experience information.

Taking advantage of blockchain technology’s immutability and time stamp functionality, SkillCheck provides users with trusted information regarding a job candidate’s education, skill, and work experience.

SkillCheck provides savings to recruiters in time and financial resources. The SkiilCheck system can also be used for additional industries, such as banking, finance, general notary service. SkillCheck is trustable as innovations in blockchain technology.

1

Time Stamped

Degree, skill level, and work experience are stored in SkillCheck with a time stamp. Information may be modified as situations change. Each version is also time stamped, thus establishing a chain of evidence.

3

Immutable

Information is entered into SkillCheck by educational, training, and other institutions directly, thus ensure such information is not tampered by third parties. Data can be amended and updated

2

Trustable

SkillCheck provides a system to store and verify education, skill, and work experience through blockchain technology. SkillCheck supports a variety of blockchain technology and storage layers.

4

Authoritative

Built on top of blockchain technology, information contained in SkillCheck is directly injected by authoritative institutions. This ensures the authenticity of such information.
